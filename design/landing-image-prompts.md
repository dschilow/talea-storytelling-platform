# Talea Landing - Nano Banana Prompts

Nutze diese Prompts fuer eine konsistente Bildwelt der Landing Page.
Bitte alle Bilder als `webp` exportieren und in `frontend/public/landing-assets/generated/` legen.

## Global Style Guardrails

- Style: premium fantasy editorial, modern children-safe product look, cinematic lighting, clean composition
- Mood: warm, hopeful, intelligent, imaginative, never kitsch
- Colors: pastel coral, soft indigo, aqua mint, warm ivory
- Rendering: ultra clean, high detail, subtle depth haze, no noisy textures
- Typography in image: none
- Logos in image: none
- Aspect ratio default: `16:9`
